## 🎮 Share Your Universe Examples

If you create interesting game worlds or universes that you'd like others to learn from:

2. **Include:**
   - Universe name & brief description
   - Publisher/Developer

   - Sample DJ names & personalities

**Examples we'd love to see:**

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I use this commercially?**
A: Yes! Deploy it in your game, SaaS product, stream, or anywhere else.

**Q: Do I have to attribute you?**
A: No legal obligation, but it helps the project if you do. 🙏

**Q: Can I remove the attribution?**
A: Legally, yes. Ethically, please don't. A small link costs nothing and helps the creator.

**Q: What if I'm a small creator with no budget?**
A: Attribution is the only "payment" we ask for. You're supporting us by spreading the word.

**Q: Can I use this in my copyrighted game?**
A: Yes! This tool is separate from game IP. Your game remains your own.

**Q: Do I need to license my generated content?**
A: The content you generate is yours. License it however you want (GPL, MIT, proprietary, etc.).
